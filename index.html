My Approach

Most language courses focus on memorizing rules and completing textbook exercises.

I focus on understanding meaning.

When you understand the underlying meaning, grammar and vocabulary become easier to remember and use naturally.

I also believe in learning through real-world materials rather than relying exclusively on textbooks.
